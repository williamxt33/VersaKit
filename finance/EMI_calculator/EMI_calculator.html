EMI Information

What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is a fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each month.

Formula: EMI = [P x R x (1+R)^N]/[(1+R)^N-1]

Where P = Principal, R = Monthly Interest Rate, N = Number of months

Factors Affecting EMI

Loan Amount: Higher loan = Higher EMI

Interest Rate: Higher rate = Higher EMI

Loan Tenure: Longer tenure = Lower EMI but more interest

Credit Score: Better score may get lower interest rates

Types of Loans

Home Loan: Typically 15-30 years, lower interest rates

Car Loan: Usually 1-7 years, moderate rates

Personal Loan: 1-5 years, higher interest rates

Education Loan: Flexible repayment, tax benefits

Tips for Borrowers

• Compare interest rates from multiple lenders

• Consider prepayment to reduce total interest

• Choose tenure based on your repayment capacity

• Maintain good credit score for better rates

• Factor in processing fees and other charges
