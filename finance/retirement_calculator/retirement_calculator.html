Retirement Planning Information

Understanding the Calculator

This calculator estimates how much you'll have saved by retirement based on current savings, regular contributions, and expected investment returns.

Note: Results are estimates. Actual returns may vary based on market conditions and investment choices.

Return Rate Expectations

Conservative (4-5%): Lower risk investments like bonds

Moderate (6-8%): Balanced portfolio of stocks and bonds

Aggressive (9-11%): Higher risk stock-heavy portfolio

Historical stock market average is about 10% before inflation.

Inflation Impact

Inflation reduces the purchasing power of money over time.

A 3% inflation rate means what costs $100 today will cost about $181 in 20 years.

Always account for inflation in retirement planning to maintain your standard of living.

Important Considerations

• This calculator provides estimates only, not financial advice

• Consider other income sources like Social Security or pensions

• Account for healthcare costs which increase with age

• Review and adjust your plan regularly

• Consult a financial advisor for personalized guidance
