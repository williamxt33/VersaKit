Mortgage Information

What is a Mortgage?

A mortgage is a loan used to purchase real estate, where the property serves as collateral.

Fixed-Rate: Interest rate remains constant throughout the loan term

Adjustable-Rate (ARM): Interest rate can change periodically

Down Payment

20% or more: Typically avoids PMI and gets better rates

Less than 20%: May require private mortgage insurance (PMI)

FHA Loans: Can be as low as 3.5% down payment

VA Loans: May offer 0% down for qualified veterans

Additional Costs

Property Tax: Varies by location, typically 0.5-2% of home value annually

Home Insurance: Protects against damage and liability

PMI: Usually 0.3-1.5% of loan amount annually

HOA Fees: For community amenities and maintenance

Tips for Borrowers

• Improve credit score for better interest rates

• Shop around with multiple lenders

• Consider total monthly payment, not just mortgage

• Factor in closing costs (2-5% of home price)

• Make extra principal payments to save on interest
