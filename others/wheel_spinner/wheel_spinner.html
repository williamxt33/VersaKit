Wheel Spinner Information

What is a Wheel Spinner?

A wheel spinner is a random selection tool divided into equal segments, each representing a choice or prize.

Mechanism: The wheel rotates and stops randomly, determining the selected segment.

Fairness: Each segment has an equal chance of being selected, unless weighted.

Common Uses

Decision Making: Choosing between options or tasks.

Games: Random rewards, Truth or Dare, or quiz selections.

Classrooms: Selecting students or topics.

Events: Prize wheels, raffles, and giveaways.

Interesting Facts

• Wheel games date back to ancient fortune devices.

• The “Wheel of Fortune” concept has existed for centuries.

• Probability depends on the number of segments.

• Some wheels use easing functions for realistic spinning.

Tips & Insights

• More segments = lower odds for each item.

• Ensure equal angles for fairness.

• True randomness requires unbiased rotation logic.

• Visual design affects perceived fairness.

Preset Templates

The spinner includes multiple preset templates for different activities and moods:

  • Dares: Fun and bold challenges for parties or games.
  • Truth: Revealing questions to get to know each other better.
  • Would You Rather: Classic dilemma questions that spark conversations.
  • Never Have I Ever: Confession-based questions perfect for groups.
  • Movie Genre: Randomly select movie categories for movie nights.
  • Video Game Genre: Pick a random gaming style for your next play session.

You can easily switch between templates or create your own custom wheel list. Each template automatically fills the wheel with relevant items for instant play.
