Dice Roller Information

Common Dice Types

D6: Standard six-sided die (1-6)

D20: Twenty-sided die for RPGs (1-20)

D100: Percentile die (1-100)

2D6: Roll two six-sided dice

Modifiers

Add a fixed number to your total roll result.

Example: 2D6 + 3 means roll two dice and add 3 to the total.

Use negative numbers to subtract from the result.

Drop Options

Drop Lowest: Remove the lowest die/dice from the result. Common in D&D character creation (4D6, drop lowest).

Drop Highest: Remove the highest die/dice from the result.

Advantage System

Advantage: Roll twice and keep the higher result. Common in D&D 5e.

Disadvantage: Roll twice and keep the lower result.

Normal: Standard single roll.
