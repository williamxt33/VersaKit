Fun Facts & Milestones

Age Facts Reference

Time Measurements:

• Year: 365.25 days (accounting for leap years)
• Month: ~30.44 days (average)
• Week: 7 days = 168 hours
• Day: 24 hours = 1,440 minutes = 86,400 seconds

Biological References:

• Average heartbeat: 60-100 beats per minute
• Breaths per day: ~20,000 (at rest)
• Cell regeneration: ~330 billion cells per day
• Sleep needed: ~1/3 of total lifetime

Age Milestones:

• 1 million hours ≈ 114 years
• 1 billion seconds ≈ 31.7 years
• 10,000 days ≈ 27.4 years
• 1 billion heartbeats ≈ 30 years
