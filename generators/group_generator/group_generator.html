Group Generator Information

How It Works

The group generator uses a random shuffling algorithm to fairly distribute people into groups.

Each person has an equal chance of being in any group.

Division Methods

Number of Groups: Specify how many groups you want.

People per Group: Specify maximum group size.

Example: 20 people → 4 groups = 5 people per group

Use Cases

• Classrooms - Assign students to project teams

• Sports - Create balanced teams for games

• Events - Organize attendees into discussion groups

• Workshops - Form breakout groups

Tips & Best Practices

• Enter clear, identifiable names

• Use balanced groups for fair team assignments

• Regenerate if you need different combinations

• Save results before generating new groups
