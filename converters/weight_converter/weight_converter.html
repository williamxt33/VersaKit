Weight Conversion Reference

Basic Weight Conversions:

1 kilogram (kg) = 1000 grams = 2.205 pounds = 35.274 ounces
1 pound (lb) = 16 ounces = 0.454 kg = 453.592 grams
1 metric ton (tonne) = 1000 kg = 2204.623 lb
1 US ton = 2000 lb = 907.185 kg = 0.907 tonne

Common Weight References:

• Paper sheet (A4): ~5 g
• Basketball: ~620 g (1.4 lb)
• Adult human: ~62-80 kg (137-176 lb)
• Car: ~1.5 tonne (3307 lb)
• Shipping container: ~2.3 tonne empty

Unit Applications:

• Gram (g): Small items, cooking ingredients
• Kilogram (kg): Human weight, retail products
• Pound (lb): Common in US/UK for personal weight
• Tonne: Shipping, industrial materials
