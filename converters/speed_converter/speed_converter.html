Speed Conversion Reference

Basic Speed Conversions:

1 m/s = 3.6 km/h = 2.237 mph = 3.281 ft/s
1 km/h = 0.278 m/s = 0.621 mph = 0.911 ft/s
1 mph = 0.447 m/s = 1.609 km/h = 1.467 ft/s
1 ft/s = 0.305 m/s = 1.097 km/h = 0.682 mph

Common Speed References:

• Average walking speed: 1.4 m/s (5 km/h, 3.1 mph)
• Usain Bolt's record: 12.4 m/s (44.7 km/h, 27.8 mph)
• Highway speed limit: 31 m/s (110 km/h, 70 mph)
• Speed of sound: 343 m/s (1,234 km/h, 767 mph)

Unit Explanations:

• m/s: SI unit of speed, used in physics & engineering
• km/h: Common road speed unit in most countries
• mph: Standard road speed unit in US & UK
• ft/s: Used in ballistics and fluid dynamics
