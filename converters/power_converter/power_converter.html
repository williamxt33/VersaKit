Power Conversion Reference

Common Power Unit Conversions:

1 kW (kilowatt) = 1,000 W (watts)
1 kW = 1.34102 HP (mechanical horsepower)
1 kW = 1.35962 PS (metric horsepower)
1 kW = 860.421 kcal/h (kilocalories per hour)
1 kW = 3,412.14 BTU/h (British thermal units per hour)

Unit Explanations:

• Watt (W): SI unit of power, equal to one joule per second
• Horsepower (HP): Imperial unit, originally meant to compare steam engine power to horses
• PS (Pferdestärke): German metric horsepower, slightly different from mechanical HP
• kcal/h: Heat power unit used in HVAC and thermal applications
• BTU/h: Common in air conditioning and heating specifications
