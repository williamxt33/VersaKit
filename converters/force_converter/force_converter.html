Force Conversion Reference

Basic Force Conversions:

1 Newton (N) = 0.102 kgf = 0.225 lbf
1 kilogram-force (kgf) = 9.807 N = 2.205 lbf
1 pound-force (lbf) = 4.448 N = 0.454 kgf
1 kilopound-force (kipf) = 4.448 kN = 453.592 kgf

Common Force References:

• Earth's gravity on 1 kg mass = 9.807 N = 1 kgf
• Average adult grip strength ≈ 300 N
• Car engine power ≈ 20-50 kN
• Rocket thrust ≈ 1000+ kN

Unit Explanations:

• Newton (N): SI unit, force needed to accelerate 1 kg at 1 m/s²
• Kilogram-force (kgf): Force of gravity on 1 kg mass
• Pound-force (lbf): Imperial unit, weight of 1 pound mass
• Kilopound-force (kipf): Used in engineering, equals 1000 lbf
