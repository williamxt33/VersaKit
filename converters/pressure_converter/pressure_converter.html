Pressure Conversion Reference

Standard Conversions:

1 atm = 101,325 Pa = 14.6959 psi = 1.01325 bar
1 MPa = 10 bar = 145.038 psi = 9.86923 atm

Liquid Column Equivalents:

Unit Explanations:

• Pascal (Pa): SI unit of pressure - force per unit area (N/m²)
• Bar: Common metric unit, equal to 100,000 Pascal
• PSI: Pounds per square inch, common in US/UK
• Atmosphere (atm): Based on average sea-level pressure
• mmHg/inHg: Mercury column height, used in barometers
• mmAq/inH₂O: Water column height, used in low-pressure systems
