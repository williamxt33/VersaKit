Angle Measurement Guide

Basic Conversions

Degrees to Other Units:

  • 1° = 60 arcminutes (')
  • 1° = 3600 arcseconds (")
  • 1° = 0.01745 radians
  • 360° = 2π radians

Radians to Other Units:

  • 1 rad ≈ 57.2958°
  • 1 rad ≈ 3437.75'
  • 1 rad ≈ 206,265"
  • π rad = 180°

Common Angular Values

Special Angles:

  • Right angle: 90° = π/2 rad
  • Straight angle: 180° = π rad
  • Full circle: 360° = 2π rad
  • Half circle: 180° = π rad

Common Angles:

  • 30° = π/6 rad
  • 45° = π/4 rad
  • 60° = π/3 rad
  • 120° = 2π/3 rad

Applications

Navigation:

  • Compass bearings (0-360°)
  • GPS coordinates (DMS)
  • Nautical navigation
  • Aviation headings

Science & Engineering:

  • Trigonometry (radians)
  • Wave physics
  • Rotational mechanics
  • Optical systems

Additional Information

DMS Format:

  • Degrees (whole circles)
  • Minutes (1/60 of degree)
  • Seconds (1/60 of minute)
  • Example: 45° 30' 15"

Precision Notes:

  • Navigation: Use DMS
  • Math: Use radians
  • Engineering: Context-dependent
  • Astronomy: Arcseconds
