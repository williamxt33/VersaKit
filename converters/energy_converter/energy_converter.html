Energy Conversion Reference

Basic Energy Conversions:

1 kJ (kilojoule) = 1000 J = 0.239 kcal = 0.948 BTU
1 kcal = 4.184 kJ = 3.966 BTU
1 kW·h = 3600 kJ = 859.845 kcal = 3412.14 BTU
1 hp·h = 2.685 kJ = 641.616 kcal = 2544.43 BTU

Common Energy Values:

• Daily adult caloric needs: ~2000-2500 kcal
• Household kW·h per day: ~30 kW·h
• Boiling 1L water: ~420 kJ
• Running 1 mile: ~100 kcal

Unit Applications:

• Joule (J): SI unit of energy, work, and heat
• Calorie (cal): Food energy and nutrition
• BTU: HVAC and heating systems
• kW·h: Electrical utility billing
